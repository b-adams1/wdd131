Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site would be to organize the books by Junji Ito. This will also add an option to search and have a brief description of the stories within them for ease of finding a specific story and which book it belongs in.

Audience

The audience is myself and others who read/are interested in reading Junji Ito's works. This will make it easier for us to locate what we are looking for.

Dynamic elements

JS would be used to grab random books to display, as well as have a search function for the books and stories. There also might be url search paramters served with JS.
